    📖 Driver's Guide

    Welcome aboard! 🏎️

    You're about to build Toyota from a tiny 1936 startup into the world's #1 automaker — and the fuel is your own focused study time. Here's the whole game in four steps:

    • Press Start and study for one Focus Lap (60 minutes by default).
    • When the lap ends, you'll hear a chime and take a Pit Stop (a 10-minute break) to rest.
    • After every few laps you earn a longer 40-minute break — you've earned it.
    • Every full hour of focus unlocks a brand-new real Toyota model and grows your sales and global rank.
    No pressure to be perfect — just press Start and take it one lap at a time. Showing up is already the hard part, and you're here. 💪

    How the focus method works

    This is a Pomodoro-style timer: you work in focused blocks separated by real breaks. Long stretches without rest drain you, so the breaks aren't a reward you have to earn — they're part of doing good work.

    • During a Focus Lap, pick one task and give it your attention. If your mind wanders, gently steer it back — that's normal.
    • When the break starts, actually step away: stretch, drink water, look out a window. Try not to scroll.
    • Prefer shorter laps? Open Settings and change Focus, Pit Stop, and Long Break to any length that suits you.
    • Tip: Spacebar starts and pauses the timer, and you can Skip a session if plans change.
    A 25-minute lap you actually finish beats a 60-minute one you dread. Set a length you can win, then build from there.

    Your growing garage

    The Garage holds 50 Toyota models, unlocked in the order they really appeared in history. Each focused hour rolls out the next one.

    • Locked slots show how many hours away they are — a clear, visible goal to drive toward.
    • Tap any unlocked car to see its year, body style, and a short note about its place in Toyota's story.
    • Watch your rank climb from 15th toward #1, while sales and revenue rise with every hour.
    • Reach 50 hours and you win the game: Toyota #1 worldwide. 🏁
    Every car in your garage is proof of time you invested in yourself. That collection only grows.

    Working with tasks

    The Tasks panel keeps your study plan in one place so each lap has a clear target.

    • Add a task and estimate how many Focus Laps it'll take — a rough guess is fine.
    • Click a task to make it active; finishing a Focus Lap adds progress to it automatically.
    • Check it off when it's done, or edit and delete tasks anytime as your plan changes.
    • The summary line gives a rough finish-time estimate so you can pace your day.
    Break big, scary tasks into small ones. "Read chapter 4" is far easier to start than "study everything."

    When it gets hard

    Some days the engine just won't turn over — and that's okay. A few things that genuinely help:

    • Start tiny. Promise yourself just five minutes. Momentum usually does the rest.
    • Rest for real. Tired focus is weak focus. Breaks and sleep make the next lap stronger.
    • Compare only to yesterday. One more hour than last time is a real win.
    • Be kind to yourself. A missed lap isn't failure — just start the next one.
    You don't have to feel motivated to begin. Press Start, and let the first lap carry you. You've got this. 🌟
